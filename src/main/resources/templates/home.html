How It Works

  1. Step 1: Find the perfect car

    Enter a location and date and browse thousands of cars shared by local hosts.

  2. Step 2: Book your trip

    Book on the RideItOut app or online, choose a protection plan, and say hi to your host! Cancel for free up to 24 hours before your trip.

  3. Step 3: Hit the road

    Have the car delivered or pick it up from your host. Check in with the app, grab the keys, and hit the road!
