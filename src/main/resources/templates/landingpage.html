Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I create an account?

To create an account, click on the "Register" button in the top-right corner. Fill in your details and follow the instructions to complete the registration process.

Can I search for vehicles without logging in?

Yes, you can search for vehicles without logging in.Simply use thr search filters on the homepage to find the perfect vehicle for your needs.

How can I list my vehicle for rent?

If your looking to list your vehicle for rent, you need to be a registered user. After logging in, navigate to your profile page and select the "Manage Your Cars" selection to add your vehicle listing.

How do I make a reservation?

Making a reservation is easy. Simply navigate to the "Reservations" section on our website, select your desired dates and location, and browse available vehicles. Once you find the vehicle you want, click "Book Now" to start the reservation process.

Is insurance included with the rental?

Basic insurance coverage is included in your rental fee. However, we offer additional insurance options for added peace of mind during your rental period.

What documents do I need to pick up the rental car?

You'll need a valid driver's license, a credit or debit card in your name for the security deposit, and a form of identification such as a passport or ID card.
